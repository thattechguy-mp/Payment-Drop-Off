Complete this quick form when leaving a check or money order in the drop box while the office is closed.

Adjust Payment Image Crop
The form will try to detect the paper edges automatically. On mobile, drag the four numbered corner markers on the image itself. If needed, scroll inside the crop window, then tap Apply Crop when the corners match the document.
If the preview area seems still, drag directly on a corner marker. On phones, the crop window now scrolls independently from the form behind it.
